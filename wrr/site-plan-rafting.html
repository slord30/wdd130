Overview

Purpose

At Dry Oar Rafting Co., our mission is to provide safe, unforgettable whitewater rafting tours that combine adventure, fun, and education. We prioritize exceptional customer service while catering to the needs of everyone: nature lovers, adventure seekers, business getaways, or family fun.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Tourists that are traveling and looking for activities to do. Travelers who enjoy adventure or nature. Businesses looking for business retreat or team-building activity. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them acessing the website? Information on whitewater rafting: it is safe, can anyone participate, is it seasonal, how much does it cost, how do I book a trip, where is it located. How will my audience usually access my site? Audience will most likely access my website by desktop or mobile. Mobile will be accessed more if audience is looking last minute.
