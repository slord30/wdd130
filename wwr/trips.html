Learn everything you need to know about white water rafting - from booking your adventure to what to expect on the river. Discover the thrill and excitement of this exhilarating outdoor activity.

Adventures

Colorado River

Experience soul-stirring scenery and some 28 rapids on one of the Colorado's steepest stretches, rivaling the big whitewater of the Grand Canyon.

Western Canyon

Travel nearly 100 river miles along the Westernmost section of the Colorado River, from Whitmore Wash to Lake Mead, experiencing both exciting rapids — including over 13 medium to low-intensity rapids for beginners — as well as smooth flowing water

Cataract Canyon

Cataract Canyon is home to the biggest whitewater rapids in Utah. You will wind through sculpted sandstone and towering red rock cliffs.

Thrilling Trips

Colorado River Full-Day Rafting

Your journey begins with a drive up one of the most scenic highways in the West. You will launch your raft onto a spectacular stretch of the Colorado River, made famous by dozens of movies shot along this stretch of the river.

Colorado River Half-Day Rafting

Sample the beauty and excitement of the Colorado River on this morning half-day adventure on mild whitewater. Raft 7 miles from Fisher Towers to Rocky Rapid.

Western Canyon Full-Day Rafting

Rated "The West's Best Short Whitewater Trip." Experience narrow canyons, rushing whitewater and distinctive geology.

Cataract Canyon 4-Day Classic

Experience an all-inclusive 4 day rafting journey through Utah's legendary Cataract Canyon just outside of Moab Utah. Exciting whitewater combined with the indescribable scenery of Canyonlands National Park make this the ideal river trip.

Cataract Canyon 2-Day Express

New high-speed, custom rafts now allow you to journey through the Colorado River’s legendary 100-mile Cataract Canyon in just 2 days. Perfect for those with busy schedules yearning for a quick, but refreshing dose of adventure.

Green River| Desolation 5-Day Rafting Duo

Winding through a remarkable wilderness of twisted mountains, dramatic cliffs, and towering plateaus carved by an ancient inland sea, the Green River through Desolation Canyon offers one of America’s favorite rafting trips.
